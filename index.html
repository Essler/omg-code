OMG!Code

What is OMG!Code?

We're a sub-specialty of the Omaha Maker Group, focused on source code. All computer programming languages are welcome.

We're a user group incubator. Excited about your bleeding edge source-code related hobby and looking for others to geek out with? Join us while you gain traction!

Getting involved

  • Join our mailing list.
  • Come to our meetings.
  • Chat with us in IRC: #omaha.dev
  • Fork our Github repo.
